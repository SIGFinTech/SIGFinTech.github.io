Share Your Talks

Every year, we host a variety of events, including numerous presentations and sharing sessions. Participating in in-person activities and discussions always sparks fresh ideas among attendees. However, we understand that many individuals cannot join in person due to various reasons. To address this, we aim to utilize the SIG-FinTech channel to offer diverse ways for everyone to share their research and make well-prepared talks accessible to a broader audience. If you have content you'd like to share, feel free to submit it through our form.

Additionally, we plan to hold online seminars periodically, offering a platform for researchers to share their insights and findings through invitations or voluntary participation. If you're interested in being a speaker at one of our seminars, please reach out to us via the form.
